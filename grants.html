How to Find and Apply for Grants

1. Know Your Mission and Needs: Define what your group stands for and what you're seeking funding for â€” such as costumes, instructor stipends, travel, food, or workshops.

2. Where to Find Grants:

  • Local: California Arts Council, Oakland Cultural Funding Program
  • Foundations: East Bay Community Foundation, Hewlett Foundation
  • National: National Endowment for the Arts, Dance/USA Fellowships
  • Databases: Candid Foundation Directory, Instrumentl, GrantWatch
  • Community Orgs: Cambodian Community Development Inc., Asian Pacific Fund

3. Track & Organize: Use this website to monitor deadlines, statuses, and requirements for each grant.

4. Build a Grant Calendar: Keep a shared calendar of due dates and reminders.

5. Prepare a Toolkit: Save templates for group bios, mission statements, budgets, letters of support, and previous proposals.

6. Write Thoughtfully: Focus on community impact, follow all instructions exactly, and keep the language clear and specific.

7. Follow Up: Thank funders if awarded. If not, ask for feedback and improve for next time.

Tips: Start with small, local grants. Reapply often. Collaborate with other organizations to strengthen applications.
