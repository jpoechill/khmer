What sound does this Khmer vowel make?

អា
[aː]
Long "a" sound
/a/ (short a)
/aː/ (long a)
/ɔː/ (long o)
/i/ (short i)
💡 Pronunciation Tip
The vowel អា makes a long "aah" sound, like the "a" in "father". Hold the sound longer than a short "a" vowel.
